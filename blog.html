The Joy of Simple Morning Coffee

By: Geraldine N.

Waking up can be tough, but the ritual of making coffee makes it all worthwhile. It doesn't have to be complicated—just a few simple steps for that perfect cup.

  1. Grind Your Beans:Freshly ground beans make a huge difference in flavor.

  2. Boil the water:Use filtered water for the cleanest taste

  3. Pour and Wait:Slowly pour the hot water over the grounds. Wait three minutes.

Enjoying this simple, warm beverage is the best way to start the day. Sometimes, the simplest things are truly the best.
